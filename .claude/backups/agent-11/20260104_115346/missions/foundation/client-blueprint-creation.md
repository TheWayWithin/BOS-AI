# Mission: Client Success Blueprint Creation

**Mission Type:** Foundation Document Creation
**Duration:** 45-90 minutes
**Output:** `/documents/foundation/client-success-blueprint.md`

---

## Mission Objective

Create a comprehensive Client Success Blueprint that defines ideal customer profiles, maps the customer journey, and establishes success metrics for each segment.

## Required Agents

- `client-success-intelligence` (primary) - Customer strategy and success mapping
- `customer-success` (support) - Customer journey expertise
- `market-intelligence` (support) - Customer research

## PHASE 0: MODE SELECTION

**Before proceeding, ask the user:**

> **How would you like to create your Client Success Blueprint?**
>
> | Mode | Description | Time |
> |------|-------------|------|
> | **Auto** | I'll create the complete blueprint based on your inputs. You review and refine at the end. | 20-30 min |
> | **Engaged** | We work section by section. I draft, present perspectives, you approve before moving on. | 45-60 min |
>
> Which mode works for you today?

**Wait for user response before proceeding.**

---

## PHASE 1: CONTEXT GATHERING

Gather essential context:

1. **Read provided context files** (PRD, market research, vision-mission)
2. **Ask clarifying questions:**
   - Who are your best customers today (or who would be)?
   - What transformation do customers experience?
   - How do customers currently solve this problem?
   - What does "success" look like for your customers?

## Success Metrics
