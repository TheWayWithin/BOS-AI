# Mission: Positioning Statement Creation

**Mission Type:** Foundation Document Creation
**Duration:** 30-60 minutes
**Output:** `/documents/foundation/positioning-statement.md`

---

## Mission Objective

Create a clear positioning statement that differentiates the business from competitors and establishes a unique market position in customers' minds.

## Required Agents

- `strategic-opportunity` (primary) - Strategic positioning
- `brand-strategy` (support) - Messaging and perception
- `market-intelligence` (support) - Competitive context

**Before proceeding, ask the user:**

> **How would you like to create your Positioning Statement?**
>
> | Mode | Description | Time |
> |------|-------------|------|
> | **Auto** | I'll create the complete positioning based on your inputs. You review and refine at the end. | 15-20 min |
> | **Engaged** | We work section by section. I draft, present perspectives, you approve before moving on. | 30-45 min |
>
> Which mode works for you today?

**Wait for user response before proceeding.**

## PHASE 1: CONTEXT GATHERING

Gather essential context:

1. **Read provided context files** (PRD, vision-mission, market research, client blueprint)
2. **Ask clarifying questions:**
   - What category do you compete in?
   - Who is your primary competitor?
   - What's your unique advantage?
   - What do you want to be known for?

## AUTO MODE EXECUTION

If user selected **Auto**:

### Step 1: Generate Complete Document

```markdown
# Positioning Statement

## Core Positioning Statement

**For** [target customer]
**who** [statement of need or opportunity]
**the** [product/service name]
**is a** [product category]
**that** [key benefit/reason to buy]
**unlike** [primary competitive alternative]
**our product** [primary differentiation]

## Category Definition

### Market Category
[What category are you creating or competing in?]

### Category Position
- [ ] Category Leader - We define the space
- [ ] Category Challenger - We're disrupting the leader
- [ ] Niche Player - We own a specific segment
- [ ] Category Creator - We're making a new category

## Competitive Differentiation

### Differentiation Matrix
| Dimension | Us | Competitor A | Competitor B |
|-----------|-----|--------------|--------------|
| [Dimension 1] | [Our approach] | [Their approach] | [Their approach] |
| [Dimension 2] | [Our approach] | [Their approach] | [Their approach] |
| [Dimension 3] | [Our approach] | [Their approach] | [Their approach] |

### Our Unique Advantage
[The one thing we do better than anyone else]

### Why It's Defensible
[Why competitors can't easily copy this]

## Value Proposition

### Primary Value Proposition
[One sentence: What value do you deliver?]

### Supporting Value Props
1. [Value 1] - [Proof point]
2. [Value 2] - [Proof point]
3. [Value 3] - [Proof point]

## Messaging Framework

### Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
[Complete pitch]

### One-Liner
[Single sentence description]

### Tagline Options
1. [Option 1]
2. [Option 2]
3. [Option 3]

## Positioning Proof Points

### Evidence
1. [Proof point 1] - [Data/testimonial/case study]
2. [Proof point 2] - [Data/testimonial/case study]
3. [Proof point 3] - [Data/testimonial/case study]

### Customer Quote (Target)
"[What you want customers to say about you]"

## Strategic Positioning Map

### Two-Axis Positioning
- X-Axis: [Dimension 1 - e.g., Price Low→High]
- Y-Axis: [Dimension 2 - e.g., Simplicity→Power]
- Our Position: [Where we sit and why]
```

### Step 2: Present for Review

Show complete document and iterate until approved.

### Step 3: Save Document

Save to `/documents/foundation/positioning-statement.md`
